Quality check · free, in-browser

Run a quality check

Goes through every entity and document and checks: link formatting, official-domain coverage, duplicate links, orphan nodes, assessment coverage, and draft/consultation items worth re-checking — then queries GOV.UK for newer sources. This can take a minute or two. Browsers can’t confirm a link is live or detect supersession outside GOV.UK, so flagged items are for your review, not auto-fixed. No cost is incurred.

Find new standards & guidance

International bodies (ISO/IEC, NIST, IEEE, ETSI, the EU, OECD, UNESCO, the Council of Europe…) each publish on their own sites, with no shared open search a browser can query directly. So this does two honest things: search every official domain at once in a new tab, or jump straight to a body’s page.
